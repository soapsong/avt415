User Scenario Mapping

Scenario:

Adeline is invited to a formal event by her mother. She realizes she has run out of some make up products and is looking for some new ones. She is looking for stand-out products and she comes across Celestial Cosmetics. She shops by categories and by collections and finds the products she needs. She adds her items to her cart, sees that it will be shipped in time for her event, and checks out.

Actor:

Adeline

Motivator:

An invitation to a formal event

Intent:

To buy some new makeup products to use for the event.

Action:

Adeline shops by categories and collections, finds the products she needs, looks at shipping times, and checks out.

Resoltion:

Adeline purchases makeup products with aesthetic packaging that works to enchance her purchasing experience.
